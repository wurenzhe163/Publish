## ğŸš€ Methodology Overview

The approach combines ascending and descending orbit SAR data to overcome geometric limitations inherent in single-orbit acquisitions. The fusion strategy aims to provide more comprehensive terrain coverage and reduce the impact of layover and shadow effects common in mountainous regions.
